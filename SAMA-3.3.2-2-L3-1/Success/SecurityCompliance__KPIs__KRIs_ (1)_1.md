## Security/Compliance  KPIs &amp; KRIs

## WHAT

- Security KPIs  will be used to track progress and improvements in relationship with security inside engineering and SSDLC.
- KRIs  are designed to monitor and provide early warnings about potential risks and threats to an organization.
- KRIs will be shared with RCC committee

WHEN

 KPIs and KRIs  will be calculated quarterly.

HOW  JIRA and Snyk/SonarQube SAST/SCA tool) will be used to get the required input data.

## WHY :

- KPIs are used to define a baseline and target improvements in key security engineering areas (planning to define security KPIs also outside engineering). The KPIs are also part of Security department OKRs.
- KRIs  are used to assess the likelihood and impact of risks and to enable proactive risk management strategies.
